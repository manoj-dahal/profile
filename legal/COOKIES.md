# Cookie Policy

**Last updated:** January 1, 2026

This site uses cookies sparingly. Here's everything you need to know.

## What Are Cookies?

Cookies are small text files stored on your device by your browser. They help us provide functionality and understand how you use the site.

## Cookies We Use

### Strictly Necessary
- **`token`** — Admin session token (only set if you sign in to the admin console)
  - Lifetime: 7 days
  - Purpose: Authentication
  - Required: No (you can browse without it)

### Functionality
- **`preferred_locale`** — Your language preference
  - Lifetime: 1 year
  - Purpose: Remember your language choice
  - Required: No

### Analytics
We use a privacy-respecting analytics setup that does **not** set cookies. All analytics are anonymous and based on hashed IP addresses.

## Cookies We DON'T Use

- ❌ Tracking cookies
- ❌ Advertising cookies
- ❌ Third-party analytics cookies (Google Analytics, Facebook Pixel, etc.)
- ❌ Cross-site cookies

## Managing Cookies

You can:
- **Clear all cookies** in your browser settings
- **Block specific sites** from setting cookies
- **Use private/incognito mode** to browse without persisting cookies
- **Use browser extensions** like Privacy Badger, uBlock Origin

If you block all cookies, the site will still work — you just won't be able to sign in to the admin console.

## Do Not Track (DNT)

We honor the DNT browser signal. If enabled, we don't track your activity across sessions.

## GDPR & ePrivacy

We are committed to GDPR and ePrivacy compliance:
- No cookies are set without consent (except strictly necessary ones)
- You can withdraw consent at any time
- We don't process personal data without a legal basis
- You have the right to data portability and erasure
