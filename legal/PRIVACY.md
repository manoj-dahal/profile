# Privacy Policy

**Last updated:** January 1, 2026

## Overview

This portfolio website ("we", "our", "us") respects your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains what information we collect, how we use it, and your rights.

## Information We Collect

### Information you provide directly
- **Contact form**: When you submit the contact form, we collect your name, email, subject, and message.
- **Account info** (if you create an admin account): username, email, password hash.

### Information collected automatically
- **Analytics**: We may collect anonymous usage statistics — page views, referrers, device type, browser type, and country (derived from IP, not stored).
- **Server logs**: IP address, user agent, request path, response code.
- **Cookies**: We use a single functional cookie for admin authentication if you sign in to the admin console.

## How We Use Your Information

We use collected information to:
- Respond to your contact form submissions
- Provide and improve the portfolio
- Analyze traffic patterns (anonymized)
- Maintain security and prevent abuse

## Data Storage

- Contact submissions are stored in a SQLite database on our server.
- Analytics data is stored in the same database, with IP addresses hashed (not stored in raw form).
- We do not sell or share your data with third parties.

## Third-Party Services

- **Google Fonts**: Used for typography. Google may log your IP when fonts are requested.
- **Hosting provider** (Vercel / Netlify / etc.): May log requests for security and analytics.
- **Email provider** (for contact notifications): Receives the contact form data you submit.

## Your Rights

You have the right to:
- **Access** — Request a copy of your data
- **Rectification** — Correct inaccurate data
- **Erasure** — Request deletion of your data
- **Object** — Object to processing
- **Portability** — Receive your data in a machine-readable format

To exercise these rights, contact: **info@manoj-dahal.com.np**

## Data Retention

- Contact submissions: 2 years
- Analytics events: 90 days
- Server logs: 30 days
- Account data: until you delete your account

## Security

We implement industry-standard security measures:
- HTTPS encryption
- Password hashing (bcrypt/scrypt)
- Rate limiting on forms
- IP hashing in analytics
- SQL injection prevention via parameterized queries
- CSRF protection on admin actions

## Children

This site is not directed at children under 13. We do not knowingly collect data from children.

## Changes

We may update this policy. Changes will be posted on this page with an updated "Last updated" date.
